What exactly is PostINNOV ?

Amidst Algeria's students unemployment crisis and the alarming brain drain phenomenon, PostINNOV emerges as a beacon of change. This dynamic platform strategically aligns academic learning with industry demands, acting as a bridge between theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
Not limited to providing internships within Algérie Poste, PostINNOV expands horizons by establishing a robust partner network, diversifying opportunities for students.
Beyond internships, PostINNOV is a hub for innovation. It supports and nurtures entrepreneurial projects through startup incubation, mentorship, and hands-on assistance. In essence, PostINNOV is not just a solution; it's a catalyst for empowering students to overcome limitations, fostering academic excellence, and driving innovation services and to contribute effectively to both local and global industries.
